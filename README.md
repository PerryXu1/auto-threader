# Auto-Threader for Twin-Hole Fiber Optics

## Overview
A device that can precisely and automatically thread thin iron electrode wire through the air hole of a twin-hole fibre through motorized mechanical movement. The initial alignment of the wire into the hole will still be performed manually. The threader will only automate the feeding step once the wire is already partially inserted.

## Background
Poling a PPSF requires threading long lengths of 50 μm iron electrode wire through an air hole of approximately 58 μm diameter in a twin-hole fibre. The tight tolerance, and the tendency of iron wire to easily buckle and kink, makes this process extremely tedious to do by hand. An automatic threader would save both time and effort during the insertion process, allowing for PPSF to be manufactured faster and more easily.
